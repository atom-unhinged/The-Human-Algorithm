# What Is THA?

## Observation

THA currently exists as a collection of canonical concepts, principles, practices, and governance, but its defining purpose remains intentionally unresolved.

The framework should not be prematurely defined by one of its possible applications (e.g. AI, education, health, governance, literature). Those are potential domains of application, not necessarily THA itself.

An open question remains:

> What unique capability is lost if a canonical note is removed?

This suggests a possible future stress test:

- Examine each canonical note independently.
- Ask what unique function it serves within THA.
- Determine whether it is foundational, derived, contextual, historical, or an application.
- Observe whether THA's identity becomes clearer through subtraction rather than addition.

Rather than deciding what THA is, allow its purpose to emerge through repeated testing.

## Open Questions

- What capability does each canonical note uniquely provide?
- Are any notes historical artifacts rather than foundational concepts?
- Does THA possess an identity independent of its applications?
- Can THA discover its own purpose through systematic stress testing?

One of the most important skills emerging in the 21st century is not coding, not critical thinking, not any single discipline — it is knowing how to interact with AI deliberately and responsibly. This is not a technical skill. It is a human skill.

How to think clearly before prompting. How to set intention rather than just query. How to filter output through [[Epistemic Hygiene]]. How to use [[AI as Cognitive Scaffold]] without outsourcing your [[The Sovereignty Principle|sovereignty]]. How to avoid generating [[Signal vs Slop]]. How to [[Manifestation as Technology|manifest outcomes]] rather than just consume information.

Current education systems are producing students fluent in the old world. [[The Human Algorithm]] is a framework under active development — one possible curriculum for the new one. It is not finished. It is being built.

Those who learn to interact with AI intentionally will not just use the tool — they will [[The Butterfly Effect as Practice|butterfly effect]] the world with it. Those who don't will be [[Memnetic|programmed]] by it without knowing.

[[AI Psychosis]] is the risk on one end. Sovereign, intentional AI use is the opportunity on the other. [[The New Literacy]] is the distance between them.
